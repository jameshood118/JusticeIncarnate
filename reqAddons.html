Justice Incarnate currently requires the following addons for raids and high level instances:

  • Omen Threat Meter
  • Ventrilo
  • Group Calendar
  • Perform AV Enabler (Allows us to Queue for AV as a group.)
  • Arena Live Frames (TCZ's) Allows easier target acquisition in arena
Optional Addons:

Please see the Roster Tutorial to learn how to update your character on the roster.

  • rpgo Character Profiler (Used for the Guild Roster)
  • PvPLog (Used for the Guild Roster)
Nifty Addons:

  • nQuest Log
    This addon allows you to see your quest log more clearly, and without all that Blizzard added junk.
  • Light Headed
    This addon actually functions functions withg nQuest Log, to show comments and other information about your quests, loaded from Wowhead.
  • MobMap Database
    This is an ingame database for looking up quests, mobs, items, recipes, etc, AND works in conjuction with nQuest Log and lightheaded
  • Recount
    Used primarily for raid leaders, but this addon gives you an insanely larger breakdown of the events over an instance than damage meters. INSANE info overload if you are not careful, do you really want to know which spells you cast got resisted the most?
  • X-perl Unitframes
    This addon is close to being an actual requirement of the guild, this contains raid frames, party frames, player frames, and gives you alot of additional information, such as cast bars, without having to resort to trying to find it in the blizzard default UI
  • Baud Bag
    I simply cannot live without my all in one bag, that doesn't do anything more than its required to... no sorting, no stacking, just simple all in one bag goodness
  • HealBot Continued
    Healbot is absolutely wonderful for fast healing. This type of healing is absolutely critical in raids. The beauty of Healbot is that you don't have to target people to heal them, and it has a built in overheal meter. You can customize it for any number of spells, including non healing spells such as Fade. It is a five star recommendation for all healers, and even non specced healers, in case healers die. Don't let the name fool you though. This is not an automated healing addon. You still have to work, it just makes that work easier.
