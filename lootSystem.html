Justice Incarnate is currently using DKP in our raids. The specific we are using assigns a value to epic drops which will be paid for with a winner's DKP. A winner is determined by having the highest DKP of all the interested parties. Ties in DKP will be determined by random rolls, or in some cases, a determination by raid leader for whom the gear would most benefit. DKP is awarded for raid attendance, boss attempts, downing bosses, and active raiding time. Negative DKP is allowed, and first time raiders are allowed to win gear.
