Welcome to the Justice Incarnate homepage! JI is an end-game raiding guild, horde-side, on Thorium Brotherhood. We are currently recruiting most classes and roles. We are currently running three Karazhan raiding groups a week, all are at the same progression. We hope to be geared out soon to run Magtheridons Lair and The Eye. Our Listing at Wowjutsu can be found Here.
March 03, 2008
Due to events outside of our control, we have decided to close up shop of JI on Kazeir's server and begin using http://justiceincarnate.guildlaunch.com please make a note and change your shortcuts.

February 11, 2008
Tonight, on the third attempt, JI officially cleared Gruul's Lair. We'd downed him before but we had a few guests to our raid. Tonight, it was all JI folks. We one shotted Maulgar and downed Gruul on the third try. Congratulations JI. Next stop, Mag's Lair!

January 13, 2008
A fresh year and a fresh kill for Justice Incarnate. Tonight we took down the second boss of Zul'aman, Akilzon, the eagle boss, despite the fact that we killed our tank right at the end! Four more bosses to go, then the timed event!
