In order to update your character's information in our roster, you must first download the rpgo Character Profiler and optionally the PvPLog. The PvPLog is used to record your PvP statistics, which are viewable on the roster.

After you have downloaded one or both of these addons, make sure you have exited World of Warcraft first so that the addons will load. Then enter WoW as usual and log into your character. Open your character sheet and you should see a save button in the top left hand corner of the character panel (by default this is opened by pressing 'C' on the keyboard). When you press save you will be asked to open your bank bags and then your professions. Simply go to your bank and open it up. For your professions open each profession window (for example tailoring or first aid). Then when you hit the save button your information will be recorded. Log out or exit World of Warcraft and then go to the roster on the website.

Find a button labeled "Update Profile" and click it. In the Upload Files box, click the browse button for CharacterProfiler.lua. Navigate to your WoW directory/WTF/Account/<account name>/Saved Variables folder. Find the CharacterProfiler.lua file and select it. This should put the path in the textbox next to the browse button. If you have PvPLog, repeat for the PvPLog.lua file. Click the Upload button at the bottom of the page. Your character's information should now be up to date in the roster. Feel free to update your character's information as necessary to help keep the roster up to date!
