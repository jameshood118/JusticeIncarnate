 Below are the news archives for Justice Incarnate for the year 2007. JI was created at the end of July 2007 so this is our first year of archives. Enjoy!

December 30, 2007
Once we get on a role, we can't stop. Tonight Justice Incarnate downed Nalorakk, the bear boss, in Zul'aman. Congrats JI!

December 29, 2007
FULL KARAZHAN CLEAR!!! A scant 5 months after JI has formed, we have done a full Karazhan clear. Considering when we started we are all mostly in greens and some blues, no purples, this is an amazing job. While we will continue farming Kara in the new year, we are also ready to start pushing forward into ZA. Congratulations on all your hard work Justice Incarnate. May the new year put us into SSC and beyond!

December 1, 2007
This evening Justice Incarnate cleared Gruul's lair, downing the big man himself in a glorious one shot try.

November 17, 2007
Justice Incarnate managed to bring down High King Maulgar, and almost had Gruul himself at 29%. Congratulations Justice Incarnate!

This morning, a couple of Justice Incarnate folks, along with our allied guild, Little Red Dot, took down the Bear boss, the Hawk boss, and the Lynx boss in Zul'Aman.
November 11, 2007
We had so much fun in Molten Core last weekend we decided to make the Old World Content a fairly regular occurence. This weekend we visited Zul'Gurub. Also nominated Jelana to be the Historian, a new rank indicating his responsibility for Old World content. Gratz Jelana!

November 4, 2007
Justice Incarnate made their first journey into Old World Content and raided MC. We didn't make it all the way through due to time constraints but we did have fun! Check out the Molten Core to see all the fun we had!

October 23, 2007
After farming Big Bad Wolf and (groan) OZ... Justice Incarnate finally saw Romeo and Juliet. With the downing of the lovers, Justice Incarnate has now OFFICIALLY cleared Opera. Way to go guys!

October 22, 2007
Sometime in the wee hours of the morning, Justice Incarnate downed Prince, leaving only Netherspite and Nightbane for a full Kara clear for Justice Incarnate!

October 19th
October seems to be a good month for Justice Incarnate. In a week of many firsts, Justice Incarnate does yet another first. Both groups have downed Illhoof. Only three more bosses to go before a full Kara clear! Awesome job Justice Incarnate!

October 18th
In an epic win, both Group 1 and Group 2 manage to down Shade of Aran, and finish off the night with their rewards at Chess.

October 17th
Justice Incarnate's second Karazhan group enters the raid and kicks butt, downing Attumen, Moroes, Maiden, and Opera in the first night.

October 11th
Aran to 4% after only a few attempts, we have only gotten to him a few times, we're moving along quite nicely, out very first attempt we got him to 50% or so

October 3rd
CURATOR DOWN! After a month of working on this bad boy Justice Incarnate finally downed him tonight after only one wipe. Congratulations Justice Incarnate!

We also fought our way through all the trash to chess for the first time tonight. Yay for free epics!

September 5th
Tonight Justice Incarnate saw and defeated part 2 out of 3 of Opera. We destroyed the Big Bad Wolf. There's no stopping us.

August 30th
Justice Incarnate finally managed to put Maiden of Justice to rest. AFTER the tanks went down! Way to go JI!

August 29th
Justice Incarnate has completed part one of three of the Opera Event. We have downed Oz! Congratulations Justice Incarnate!

August 17th
It took us a couple of weeks, but we managed to down Moroes! Go go Justice Incarnate!

July 31st
Justice Incarnate told Attumen who was boss.
